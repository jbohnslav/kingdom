# Closing a backlog ticket should move it to archive

When a backlog ticket is closed (status changed to closed), it should automatically be moved from .kd/backlog/tickets/ to .kd/archive/backlog/tickets/. Currently tickets stay in place and still show up in 'kd tk list'.
